Hi, I'm Shuo.

My name is Shuo Song. I am a PhD student at the Toulouse School of Economics, France. My research interests lie in Empirical Industrial Organization. My current work focuses on the IO of GenAI. Specifically, I study the competition among LLMs and the associated infrastructure sectors, such as semiconductors and energy.

Prior to joining TSE, I obtained my Bachelor's degree from the Wang Yanan Institute for Studies in Economics (WISE) at Xiamen University, China. Outside of economics, I cultivate interests in photography and the history of Ming Empire (1368-1644).

My favorite poet is XIN Qiji.
